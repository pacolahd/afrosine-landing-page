About Our App

Make your food sweeter...

Afrosine is an innovative mobile application designed to generate and customize traditional African recipes based on user inputs, such as location and available ingredients. With a comprehensive database of African cuisines, Afrosine captures regional variations and traditional cooking methods, offering users an authentic culinary experience. Developed using an incremental, agile-driven software model, the application ensures iterative progress and flexibility, allowing it to adapt to user feedback and evolving requirements. Initial testing has demonstrated promising results, indicating strong user engagement and high levels of satisfaction.

Amazing Features

Afrosine, make your food sweeter...

Personalized Recipe Recommendation

Discover recipes tailored to your taste preferences and dietary needs with our smart recommendation system.

Intuitive Search and Filter

Enter the ingredients you currently have in your pantry, and let our app suggest delicious recipes you can make with them. Avoid food waste and get inspired by what’s already in your kitchen.

Interactive Recipe Cards

View detailed recipe cards with step-by-step instructions, nutritional information, and mouth-watering images.

Ingredient-Based Recipe Generator

Easily find recipes by ingredients, cuisine, dietary restrictions, or meal type using our comprehensive search and filtering options.

Recipe Customization

Adjust ingredient quantities, swap out ingredients, or modify cooking steps to fit your preferences or dietary needs. Our app helps you personalize each recipe to your liking.

Favorite Meals

Easily bookmark and save your favorite recipes to your personal profile. Access them anytime for quick reference, meal planning, or re-creation of delicious dishes you love.
